Chatbot_Powerlook.ipynb — AI Chatbot for Powerlook India
Introduction
A multi-feature AI-powered chatbot system built for Powerlook, an Indian men's fashion brand, combining rule-based responses with ML components.
Dataset Used
Synthetic/sample customer data created within the notebook (no external CSV).
Features / Modules

Rule-based Chatbot - handles FAQs on orders, returns, products, and shipping via intent matching.
Customer Churn Prediction - Random Forest Classifier trained on customer attributes (Age, Tenure, Total Spend, etc.) to identify at-risk customers.
Recommender System - suggests products based on past purchase categories.
Sentiment Analysis - uses TextBlob to detect customer sentiment from reviews and respond with positive/neutral/negative-specific recommendations.
Lead Scoring - classifies customer leads by acquisition source (Instagram, Google Ads, Email, etc.) to prioritize sales outreach.

Conclusion
The project goes beyond a simple chatbot to build a full customer intelligence suite. By combining NLP, classification, and recommendation logic, it provides Powerlook with tools for customer retention, personalized marketing, and lead prioritization.
